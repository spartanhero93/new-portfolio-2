Who I am

Ever since I was a little one I had always had a fascination with electronics, from messing with computers to taking apart TVs. But throughout the years I wasn't exactly sure which of the many careers that I could take was the one for me. During and after high school I began to take simple jobs, so I could able to afford the things I needed when I was attending college. In an effort to attempt to escape the status quo that I was currently in, I began experimenting with software development, I immediately fell in love with the ability to create anything within my reach, without having to break my back or muscles in the process. I had tinkered with computers early on in my life, but on a hardware level, from building PC's for family members, to building gaming computers, and windows servers.
